About Us

For more than 10 years, Ariya Thai Kitchen has been providing the people of Huntington Park incredible, flavorful Thai food. Since the moment the doors opened to today, Ariya has focused on providing fresh food in the most cleanest of environments as proven with our A grade from the Health Department.
